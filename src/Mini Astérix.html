We are Mini Astérix, the educative project team within the Carbon team!

Our eight members have various profils : first and second year bachelor and master students. Yet they share their will of getting accustomed to prototyping!

Mini Astérix is indeed a smaller version of Astérix which is used as a test bench to perform fluid dynamics tests. Our team's main goal is to improve the machine and prepare for the next tests that will be done during the following semster as part of a semster project.

In the Carbon team, no experience doesn't mean that you can't play a crutial role in the prototype development!
