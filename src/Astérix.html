Astérix, Obélix and Idéfix from the comic "Astérix le Gaulois" originally written by René Goscinny, illustrated by Albert Uderzo and first pulbished in 1959 at the "Hachette" editions.

We are Astérix, the team in charge of the first module within the Carbon team!

The seven members forming the team are working on the development of part of the prototype called "Astérix". As you might already ghave discovered it, this module uses adsobants to capture CO2 from the raw atmosphere. This is the first part of the direct air capture process. The purified air flux is released back in the atmosphere whereas the CO2 enriched flux is destined to Idéfix via going through Pneumatix.

Our main goals are : improving the machine's performance and redining the code to make it easier to understand and better suited for use.

We are here make this CO2 capture project come true!
