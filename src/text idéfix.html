We are Panoramix, the chemistry team within the Carbon team!

Comprising nine passionate members, we bring together a diverse mix of expertise from BA3 students to PhD researchers, spanning various fields : Chemistry (CGC), Material Science (MX) and Environmental Science (SIE).

We collaborate closely with our partner labs, the Laboratory of Advanced Separations (LAS) and the Laboratory for Functional Inorganic Materials (LFIM), tackling everything from simulations and process modeling to the quest for the prefect adsorbent.

New adsorbants are being disovered and perfected every day to optimize key componenets. This quest is a case of finding a balance between key paramters. The ideal adsorbant should of course have a great adsoption capacity and high selectivity (targenting only CO2 molecules).
